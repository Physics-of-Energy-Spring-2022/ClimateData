The CO2 and Greenhouse Gas Emissions dataset includes information about historical emissions curated by Our World in Data


owid-co2-data.csv contains the a table of the data with rows containing yearly emissions data for a country 

owid-co2-codebook.csv includes information about what each column of the dataset represents and where the data was acquired from. 
For example, by looking in the row "methane", you can see that the methane column of owid-co2-data.csv represents 
"Total methane emissions including land use change and forestry, measured in million tonnes of carbon dioxide-equivalents" and was obtained from data provided by 
the "CAIT Climate Data Explorer via. Climate Watch"
